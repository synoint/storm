Syno Survey - Embedding Instructions

Basic Usage

Firstly, include the Syno Survey embedding script in the <head> of your page 

                            
<html>
 <head>
  ...
  <script src="https://survey.synointcdn.com/embed/v1/script.min.js"></script>
  ...
 </head>
 <body>
 ...

Then place the following code into the body of the page to render survey invitation dialog 

                            
                                
<div id="survey-container-id"></div>...
                                
<script>
 synoES.init({
  'containerId': 'survey-container-id',
  'surveyURL': 'https://survey-provider-example-domain.com/en/s/12345',
  'titleText': 'ðŸ‘‹ Hi, do you have 60 seconds to help us improve our experience?',
  'hintText': 'Donâ€™t worry, all responses are anonymous.',
  'buttonLabel': 'Next',
  'buttonColor': 'rgb(224, 104, 145)',
  'containerColor': 'rgb(51, 51, 51)',
  'skipURLs': ['https://example.com/checkout','https://www.example.com/cart'],
  'invitationTimeoutSeconds': 60,
  'surveyPoolSizePercent': 10,
  'surveyPoolExpirationDays': 30,
  'enableTracking': false,
  'supportURL': 'https://synoint.com',
  'supportText': 'If you have questions contact our support'
});
</script>
...

containerId - ID of the parent element where survey will be inserted

surveyURL - live public URL for Syno Survey

skipURLs - survey invitation will not be shown on these URLs

invitationTimeoutSeconds - survey invitation will not be shown at once but after 'invitationTimeoutSeconds' passed

surveyPoolSizePercent - survey invitation will be shown only to certain specific percent of users (browsers)

surveyPoolExpirationDays - used in conjunction with 'surveyPoolSizePercent' setting. Specify amount of days user will stay in (or out of) invittion pool.

enableTracking - is 'true' by default if nothing set. Enabled events tracking like invitation popup shown.

supportURL and supportText - both are required to see 'support' block.
